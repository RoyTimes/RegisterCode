Extracurricular Infomation
Extracurricular Type:

Student Council: only official student body representative in the school, or xueshenghui.

Student Club: official student club sponsored by the school with a leadership team and active member base. Have a general club mission and continues club activities.

Student Project: unofficial projects created and organized by the student for a given mission within a given time period. Project will have a final deliverable and project will terminate after the release of the deliverable.
