4. Annual Report


Program:    Student Organizations of Excellence(SOE)


Annual Report Guidance

Student activity annual report is a critical componenet for an activitiy (student council/club/project) to showcase its leadership strucutre, governance, and achievement. Therefore, it is a required componenet for our registration. Below are some information we ask you to include in your report:

1) Brief history of the activity (when was it founded, for what purpose, how long has it been operational, and etc)


2) Current status of the activity (# of members, current progress on its mission, relationship with the school/larger community)


3) Financial status of the activity (Where did you get funding? Any fundraising activities? How much budget the activity has?)


4) Member selection process and internal promotion rules (how are leaders of the club getting promoted/selected)


5) List of current leadership


6) Frequency of all activity member meetings & leadership meetings


7) Frequency and content of leadership trainings within the activity


8) Introduction of signature activity output (big events, reports, performances, and etc) with details on the output's impact


9) Activity's plan for the future
