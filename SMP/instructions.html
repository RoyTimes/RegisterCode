What will we do with your school profile?
We understand that branding your school professionally and effectively to so many international universities can be challenging and exhausting. Through the school profiling on SMP, your school profiling will be improved with professional guidance and tailored strategies. It will also be part of our combined school branding campaigns to overseas universities this summer. Your information here will be the primary source of school profiling for admission officers. We plan to start this year's high school branding trip to international colleges from March to September 2016.


How will we help you to complete your school profile?
We have carefully designed a school profile template for you to complete and fill in necessary information. We will also offer free service to help you analyze your raw data and input information into the template if information the template asks is not readily available in your school yet. School representatives are also more than welcome to contact us directly to get more help and guidance on profile completion.
